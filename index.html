MUN Draft Resolution Builder

How to Use This Builder:

  1. Fill in the header information (committee, topic, etc.)
  2. Add preambulatory clauses (statements of fact, acknowledgments)
  3. Add operative clauses (actions to be taken)
  4. Add signatory countries
  5. Preview and copy your draft resolution

Tip: Click on phrases in the phrase banks to insert them into your clauses.
